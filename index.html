Strike Shoes: Smart Footwear for Diabetic Ulcer Detection

Leveraging technology to improve diabetic foot health through early detection and real-time monitoring.

The Problem & Solution

Diabetic foot ulcers significantly impact health, leading to serious complications such as infections and amputations due to delayed detection.

Our Solution

  • Temperature, Sweat, Ph ,and odor sensors for early detection.
  • Continuous foot pressure monitoring.
  • Vibration feedback to alert users about risks.
  • Mobile app connectivity for real-time alerts.

Technology

Hardware

  • Flexi-force pressure sensors.
  • Odor and sweat sensors for infection detection.
  • ESP32-based microcontroller for real-time processing.

Software

  • AI/ML models for early ulcer prediction.
  • Cloud-based storage for health tracking.
  • Mobile app for alerts and doctor consultations.

Market & Business Model

Market Opportunity

  • Global diabetic population: ~537 million
  • Diabetic Foot Ulcer Treatment Market: $11.2 billion by 2026
  • Targeting hospitals, clinics, and telemedicine services

Business Model

  • Direct B2C sales of smart shoes.
  • B2B partnerships with hospitals and insurance companies.
  • Subscription-based AI health tracking service.
